What's so different about us?

We specialize in Residential metal roofing and we can proudly say that we are the best at it. We are the only roofing company in Edmonton who focus exclusively on high quality metal. We are always up to date with the latest installation technologies available. We - concentrate all our knowledge and experience on your roof; You - receive best possible results worry-free.

We are safety oriented company. Period. Our every worker is certified in highest safety standards. Each crew has at least one First Aid person. So, it is safer when we are next to your family. We became COR certified and in excellent state with WCB.

All roofs are unique. This is why our specialists are there to help select the best product that suits your roof. We always aim to educate our clients. We realize that your next roof is a big investment and the last roof you will ever install. That is why it has to be simply perfect every time you look at it.
